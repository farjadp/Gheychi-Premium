Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

At Gheychi Premium, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our Telegram bot and Web application.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data required to provide our service:

  • Telegram User ID (to identify your account and subscription status)
  • Links submitted for processing (these are processed temporarily and not stored permanently)
  • Basic usage analytics (number of downloads, bot interactions)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information solely to deliver the media downloading service, process payments (via our third-party provider), and improve the reliability of our systems.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share necessary data with third-party service providers (like payment processors) strictly for fulfilling their services.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@gheychipremium.com.
